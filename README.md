## 🧭 My Journey

It started with a degree in **Informatics Engineering** from UPN "Veteran" Yogyakarta (Cum Laude, 2018) — where I fell in love with how data could tell a story.

Fresh out of university, I joined **PT Bank Mandiri** through PT Anugrah Karya Abadi, where I got my hands dirty building ETL pipelines with Talend and SQL Server. One of my proudest early wins: **automating financial reports that cut manual effort by 80%**. That moment taught me that good data engineering isn't just technical — it creates real business impact.

That momentum carried me to **PT Bank BTPN Syariah** (via PT Avows Technologies), where I've spent the last 4+ years architecting enterprise-scale data solutions. From migrating on-premise data warehouses to AWS, to building Tableau dashboards that help stakeholders make smarter decisions — I've learned that data pipelines are only as good as the insights they unlock.

Today, I specialize in **end-to-end data engineering** — from raw ingestion to cloud warehousing to BI — with a focus on the banking and financial sector.
